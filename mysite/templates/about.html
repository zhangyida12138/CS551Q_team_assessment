About Page


This App is based on open data sourced from World Development Indicators, and it aims to display Rural population (% of total population) data set for various Countries visually.


Rural population refers to people living in rural areas as defined by national statistical offices. It is calculated as the difference between total population and urban population.


To compare countries, input the Country Code for a country. Country Code can be found on the View Countries page and it's a 3-letter words like 'ABW' for the country - Aruba.


To compare countries in a specific region, input a number in the Region ID: field corresponding to a region in the list below

  1. 1 - Latin America & Caribbean
  2. 2 - No region
  3. 3 - South Asia
  4. 4 - Sub-Saharan Africa
  5. 5 - Europe & Central Asia
  6. 6 - Middle East & North Africa
  7. 7 - East Asia & Pacific
  8. 8 - North America

To compare countries in an income group, input a number in the Income Group ID: field corresponding to an income group in the list below

  1. 1 - High income
  2. 2 - No group
  3. 3 - Low income
  4. 4 - Lower middle income
  5. 5 - Upper middle income

You can navigate to Home page.
